Everything in the blockchain made easy

Access your Digital assets across multiple networks with a secure crypto Wallet.

The Scallop Extension is a multi-chain crypto wallet that serves as a gateway to thousands of web3 decentralized applications (dApps).

The Scallop Wallet Extension supports both fiat & crypto and has proper multi-chain support for Ethereum and all EVM chains such as Polygon and Binance Smart Chain.

You can use the Scallop wallet extension to:

  • Transform your desktop browser into a powerful self-custody web3 wallet and portal to thousands of decentralized applications in an instant.
  • Safely store and receive cryptocurrency from family and friends as well as your exchange account.
